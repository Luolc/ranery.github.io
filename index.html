About Me

I am a senior undergraduate of Huazhong University of Sci&Tech (HUST), majoring in Electronic and Information Engineering. Currently I am a research intern advised by Dr. Yu Cheng. Before that, I spent two years in Vision and Learning Representation Group, one of the best artificial intelligence laboratories in China, for accumulating machine learning related knowledge under the instruction of Prof. Wenyu Liu.

My research interests are the general artificial intelligence and machine learning in both theory and application. I am also interested in applying mathematical models for solving real-world problems. Beyond undergraduate studies, I have participated in various interdisciplinary contest in modeling, that will be elaborated in Projects.
